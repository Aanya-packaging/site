ABOUT US

AANYA PACKAGING INDUSTRIES Manufacturing of HIgh Quality Corrugated Boxes, an ISO 9001:2015 AND Forest Stewardship Council (FSC) Certified Company, is a sole proprietorship firm which came into existence in 2016 in Faridabad which is sister concern of A-One Packaging Company (Since 2012). Since inception the firm has been deals in Corrugated Boxes, Packaging Material like Polythene, Silicon Gel, Box Strips, and other accessories.

Under the control and guidance of Mr. Amit Ambavta, Proprietor, the firm has touched new skies in past and rapidly growing by passing of time. It had set up its manufacturing units and office in Faridabad.

Over the years it has catered to various customers like Mitra Industries Pvt. Ltd., Bohra Rubber Pvt. Ltd., Imperial Auto Industries, MDH Spices, Nichirin Imperial Auto Parts India Pvt. Ltd., Tokai Imperial India Pvt. Ltd., R-Pure Pvt. Ltd., and number of smaller units in and around Faridabad.

From the very beginning the thrust of the firm has been a packing concept rather than supplies in order than the end product is packed in the best possible way at the lower cost.
