AANYA PACKAGING INDUSTRIES




Established in the year 2013, we “Aanya Packaging Industries” is a manufacturer of the wide spectrum of packaging boxes, corrugated rolls, carton boxes, etc. We manufacture these products using premium-grade raw material procured from authentic vendors. Our products are offered at reasonable rates and delivered within the promised time-frame.

We have hired an adroit team of employees that stays abreast of advanced manufacturing techniques and designs. Furthermore, we have a quality control unit where we check our entire range on defined parameters like design, quality, and finish. All units are equipped with essential tools, machines, and technology to manufacture a high-quality range of products.

Under the valuable guidance of our mentor, Mr. Amit Ambavta, we are growing at a notable rate in the market. With rich industrial experience, he enables us to understand the varied requirements of our clients.
